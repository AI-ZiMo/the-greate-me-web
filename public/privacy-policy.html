Privacy Policy for The Great Me

Effective Date: November 29, 2025

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to The Great Me ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and web application.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Developer: 蒋政涛 (Jiang Zhengtao)
  • Email: jiangzhengtao02@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Task Content: We collect and store content you create through our service:

  • Task descriptions and titles
  • Task completion status
  • Task start and end times
  • Language preference (Chinese or English)

AI Chat Messages: When you use the AI assistant feature, we collect:

  • Your chat messages and questions
  • Task context shared with the AI to provide relevant assistance

Important: We do not collect any account information. The Great Me does not require user registration, and we do not collect email addresses, passwords, usernames, or any other personal identifiers.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Local Device Storage:

  • All your tasks and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage (mobile) or browser local storage (web)
  • We do not collect or store this data on our servers

No Analytics or Tracking:

  • We do not use analytics services
  • We do not track your usage patterns
  • We do not collect device information, IP addresses, or location data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core functionality: Store and manage your tasks on your device
  • Enable AI assistance: Send your chat messages and task context to AI service providers to provide intelligent task management assistance
  • Text-to-speech: Convert text to speech for narration features using Aliyun TTS service
  • Language preferences: Remember your language choice (Chinese or English)
  • Improve user experience: Maintain your tasks and settings across app sessions

3. How We Share Your Information

Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

Aliyun (阿里云) - Text-to-Speech Service

  • Purpose: Converts text to audio for narration features
  • Data Shared: Text content from the app and your language preference
  • Privacy Policy: Aliyun Privacy Policy

OpenAI-Compatible AI Service

  • Purpose: Provides AI-powered task management assistance
  • Data Shared: Your chat messages, current task list, task statistics, current time and date
  • Note: The actual AI service provider depends on your configuration. This may be OpenAI, Google Gemini, or other compatible services.

What We DO NOT Share

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use your data for marketing purposes
  • We do not share your task data with any third parties except the service providers listed above

4. Data Retention

Local Storage: Your task data and preferences are stored on your device until you manually delete them. We do not automatically delete your data.

Cloud Services: Text sent to Aliyun TTS and AI service providers is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored by us. Please refer to the respective service providers' privacy policies for their data retention practices.

Data Deletion: You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual tasks within the app
  • Clearing the app data in your device settings (mobile)
  • Clearing browser data/local storage (web)
  • Uninstalling the app (mobile)

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and is always accessible to you
  • Correction: You can update or correct your task information directly in the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual tasks or clear all app data at any time
  • Data Portability: Your data is stored in standard JSON format in local storage
  • Opt-Out of AI Features: You can choose not to use the AI assistant feature. The app will function normally without it.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Local Storage Encryption: Your data is stored using platform-standard secure storage mechanisms
  • HTTPS Communications: All communications with third-party services use encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No Central Database: We do not maintain a central database of user information, reducing the risk of data breaches
  • API Key Protection: Service provider API keys are stored securely and never exposed to users

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

The Great Me is available to users worldwide. When you use our AI assistant and text-to-speech features, your data may be transferred to and processed by:

  • Aliyun (阿里云): Servers located in China
  • AI Service Providers: Server locations depend on the configured provider (may include US, EU, or other regions)

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and displaying a notification in the app for significant changes.

By using The Great Me, you agree to this privacy policy.
